## Team Motivation Behind LCA-I

As a team, we were motivated by one shared problem: high-quality LCA is important, but the process is still too slow, expensive, and difficult for many companies.

Fang Ze brought the environmental engineering perspective, Kian Hwee highlighted the ESG and business decision-making gap, and I focused on how technology could reduce manual workload. Together, we wanted to build a practical solution that helps teams go from scattered product data to impact insights and reporting much faster.

---

## How LCA-I Works

LCA-I is designed to feel simple for users while handling the heavy work in the background:

1. Users upload product information (for example BOM and process data).
2. The system checks for missing fields and prompts for clarifications.
3. Agent workflows map inputs to relevant LCA datasets and impact factors.
4. The pipeline computes key impact metrics and identifies major hotspots.
5. LCA-I generates visual summaries and a report for faster decision-making.

This workflow helps companies move from raw operational data to practical ESG insights in hours instead of months.
